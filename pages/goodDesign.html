There are many ways to fix the issues of the iphone timer. Here is one of the ways I believe would fix the interface issue of the timer. This interface doesn't require a scroll wheel to select the time you can just click on the buttons you want to make the timer right.

How many hours fo you want on the timer?


How many minutes do you want on the timer?


As you can see from the prototype above. My improved version of the iPhone Timer would work like a microwave but instead of minutes and seconds its hours and minutes. The user can select multiple buttons to choose the time they wish to put on the timer. So if you wanted a 10 hour and 27 minute timer, you would select buttons 1 and 0 for the hours and then 2 and 7 for the minutes. I believe that with this design this would allow for a greater user experience and overall improvement to the original design.
